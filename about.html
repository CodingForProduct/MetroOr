MetroOrDrive

MetroOrDrive came to be because of a five week coding workshop, Coding For Product, which is the brainchild of the awesome Wai-Yin Kwan, in coordination with Philosophie and Women and Code. This amazing workshop walked coders of various skill levels through the app cycle from idea to design to language and framework choices to user testing, all the way to deployment.

The focus was to be the Metro system in Los Angeles and the various teams came up with some pretty wonderful and varied apps.

Our team created a tool that computes your annual average commute in both time and cost to and from work for public transportation and driving.

With this tool, commuters can decide which makes the most sense for them: Metro or drive?
